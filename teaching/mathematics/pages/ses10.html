Session 10: Solving trigonometric equations

This session looks at plotting trigonometric functions in EXCEL, and if given such a graph, how can we identify the parameters of the corresponding sinusoidal function? We will also learn how to solve trigonometric equations, which involves finding multiple solutions due to the periodic nature of such functions.
